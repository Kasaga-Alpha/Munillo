Version What is Munilo?

Munilo is a community of travellers and travel agencies for various modes of transport ranging from road, air, water and railway. The platform connects the two groups to ease booking of tickets and making of payments. Munilo selects travel agencies that fulfil the required standards to ensure that travellers get the best services.
