Help & Feedback Sign in to Munilo

You can log into your application using either the local account you signed up with, or a google account, or facebook account on the login page.

Forgotten password

To reset your password, hit the label titled "Forgot Password? Get Your password" which will redirect you to the password reset service on the login page.

 Sign Up with Munilo

To open an account with Munilo, hit the "Sign up" button on the login page to get started.

Payments

The client should be able to make full payments using the Munilo platform three (3) hours prior to the trip start schedule time. A history for any payments made before by the client are displayed in the payments section that can be accessed using the link "payments" in the side menu.

Service providers

All service providers wishing to join the platform should register their organisations and specify their user types while creating local accounts in the "SIGN UP" section.
